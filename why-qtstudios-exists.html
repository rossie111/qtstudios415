Why QT Studios Exists: Building a Radical Space for Q/T/BIPOC Artists

by Rossie

10/8/2025

A Space Born from Rebellion

In a world where queer, trans, and BIPOC (Q/T/BIPOC) artists are routinely marginalized, silenced, and excluded from mainstream creative industries, QT Studios was built as an act of resistance.

Gentrification, systemic racism, transphobia, and the economic fallout of COVID-19 have stripped away many of the spaces where radical, anti-capitalist art could thrive. For too long, Q/T/BIPOC artists have been forced to navigate an industry that undervalues their voices, underfunds their work, and dismisses their stories.

QT Studios exists to change that.

We are more than just a hybrid music rehearsal and video production space—we are a revolutionary incubator for artists who refuse to conform. Our mission is to dismantle white supremacy, settler colonialism, and capitalist exploitation through art, music, and unapologetic activism.

Our Mission: Art as Liberation

At QT Studios, we believe that creativity is a powerful tool for social change. Our mission is simple but radical:

  • Provide Q/T/BIPOC artists with the space, resources, and community they need to thrive.

  • Foster a non-hierarchical, anti-oppressive environment where experimentation and collaboration are prioritized over profit.

  • Amplify marginalized voices through cutting-edge artistic expression and political activism.

  • Inspire the next generation of radical creatives to challenge the status quo.

We reject the idea that art should be apolitical or sanitized for mass consumption. Instead, we embrace the messy, bold, and revolutionary nature of Q/T/BIPOC creativity.

Why QT Studios Matters

The arts and culture sector is still dominated by systems that exclude marginalized voices. Mainstream studios, rehearsal spaces, and production houses often cater to those who already have access—leaving Q/T/BIPOC artists struggling to find safe, affordable, and affirming environments to create.

QT Studios fills that gap.

We are a sanctuary for those who have been told their art is "too political," "too niche," or "not marketable." Here, your art is not just welcome—it’s essential.

By centering Q/T/BIPOC artists, we:

  • Disrupt the white, cisheteronormative standards of the creative industry.

  • Empower artists to explore their craft without compromise.

  • Build a legacy of inclusivity and radical creativity.

What Makes Us Different

QT Studios isn’t just another rehearsal space or production studio. We are a cultural and political vanguard—where art meets activism, and creativity fuels liberation.

  1. We Center the Marginalized

    2. While other spaces cater to the mainstream, we exist specifically for Q/T/BIPOC artists. Our priority is your vision, your voice, and your freedom—not profit or prestige.

  2. We Embrace Radical Politics

    3. We don’t shy away from the fact that art can—and should—be a form of protest. From livestreamed performances to collaborative projects, we support work that challenges oppression.

  3. We Foster Community

    4. This isn’t just a studio—it’s a movement. We believe in collective power, mutual aid, and the strength of queer, trans, & BIPOC kinship.

  4. We’re Building the Future

    5. By investing in Q/T/BIPOC artists today, we’re ensuring that the next generation has a blueprint for revolutionary creativity.

Join the Revolution

QT Studios is more than a space—it’s a statement. A declaration that Q/T/BIPOC artists deserve resources, visibility, and power.

If you’re an artist who’s been searching for a place to create without limits, to collaborate without compromise, and to make art that shakes the foundations of oppressive systems—this is your home.

Welcome to the rebellion.
